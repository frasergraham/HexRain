Need help, found a bug, or have feedback? Email twistedweasel@icloud.com and we'll get back to you.

Privacy Policy

Hex Rain does not collect personal information. We don't ask for your name, email, location, contacts, or any other identifying details to play the game.

What we do collect

  • Anonymous gameplay analytics. We count aggregate events such as how many runs are started and what score range each run ends in, using GoatCounter. GoatCounter does not use cookies and does not collect IP addresses or any data that could identify you.
  • Local device storage. Your high score, chosen difficulty, and which one-time tutorials you've already seen are kept in your device's local storage. They never leave your device.
  • Game Center (iOS only). If you sign into Game Center, your scores and achievement progress are stored by Apple under Apple's privacy policy. Hex Rain does not see your Game Center identity beyond what Apple exposes for leaderboard ranking.

What we don't do

  • No advertising, no advertising identifiers, no ad tracking.
  • No third-party data sharing or selling of any kind.
  • No account, sign-up, or login.

Changes

If this policy changes we'll update this page. Material changes will be noted in the app's release notes.
